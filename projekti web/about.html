About Us

Peshkatari.ks is a passionate community for nature and fishing, created to unite all enthusiasts of this sport in a single, modern, and open platform.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and unite the fishing community through activities, useful information, and mutual support. To promote responsible fishing and a love for nature.

  • We organize regular fishing competitions and events
  • We offer advice, training, and educational articles
  • We promote nature conservation and sustainable fishing
  • We create space for sharing photos and stories

Our Values

  • Respect for nature and the environment
  • Collaboration and mutual support
  • Transparency and integrity
  • Creativity and innovation

Join our community!

Register, participate in events, and share your passion with us. Together we create new memories on every fishing trip!
